Annabell's Exchange

Annabell's Exchange is a 3D telephone operator sim in which the player determines the narrative's progression by the manner in which they connect, disconnect, and/or fail to connect any of a number of telephone calls which come through the telephone exchange's switchboard. Its silent-film-era presentation (from its intertitle-influenced UI to its damaged and vignetted film aesthetic) lends an air of authenticity to its representation of the Great Depression.
